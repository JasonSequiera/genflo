Track your cycle and symptoms

About Us

Why We Built GenFlo

GenFlo was born from a simple truth — women's health has been underfunded, understudied, and underrepresented in technology for too long. We set out to change that.

Our platform brings together cutting-edge hormonal science, AI, and compassionate design to give every person a tool that genuinely understands the rhythm of their body — not just a generic health app, but a true companion through every phase.

We believe that understanding your cycle is not just about tracking dates — it's about unlocking a deeper connection to your energy, emotions, skin, nutrition, and relationships.
